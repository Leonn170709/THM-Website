The Highway Men (THM) is the major highway clan on the anarchic Minecraft server 6b6t, founded on October 19, 2024 by mingailas. After the disbandment of the Highway Extension Group, THM stepped up to fill the void.

What started as a small group focused on the Nether dimension quickly grew into the largest highway infrastructure organization on the server. At peak activity, THM workers cleared over 100,000 blocks daily on average.

7 months after its creation, the clan reached 500 Discord members with 10–20 active workers consistently pushing highways toward the world border.

THM established the universal "5 Blocks Wide" rule on Nether highways in March 2025, a standard now followed across the server for efficient, maintainable highway construction.

Timeline

History

October 19, 2024

Foundation: "Moon"

Player mingailas founded a clan called Moon. A few days later it was rebranded to The Highway Men after realizing the potential in highway work. First members included CapyKing10, CaT_345, Volizray, xm4nn_, and others.

Late 2024

Rapid Expansion

Focusing on the Nether dimension, the group cleared 100K blocks daily on average. THM became the de facto successor to the disbanded Highway Extension Group, gaining rapid recognition on the server.

February 2025

TNT Dupe Discovery

Core member Ooranges published a video showcasing a TNT duplication exploit. The dupe was patched roughly 12 hours after going public, one of the shortest-lived dupes in 6b6t history.

March 2025

The 5 Blocks Wide Rule

Negotiations between THM and The Watchmen resulted in the universal adoption of the "5 Blocks Wide" standard on all Nether highways. This became a defining moment in organized 6b6t infrastructure.

Mid 2025

500 Discord Members

7 months after founding, the THM Discord hit 500 members. 10–20 active workers were consistently extending highways, solidifying THM's dominance in 6b6t highway infrastructure.

2025–Present

Highway Sabotage Collective (HSC)

The Highway Sabotage Collective emerged as the primary antagonist group, blocking several thousand blocks at a time for weeks. THM persisted and continued pushing highways toward the world border regardless.

The 5 Blocks Wide Rule

5

Universal Highway Standard

Negotiated between THM and The Watchmen in March 2025, the 5 Blocks Wide Rule became the enforced highway standard on all main Nether highways. Controversial at first, it quickly proved its value in practice.

Cost Efficiency5 blocks per unit is cheaper than 6, allowing faster extension to the world border.
Griefing ResistanceHarder to sabotage than 4-wide, but easier to restore than 6-wide.
NavigationA clean center-line for elytra and foot travel, fits 2 players side by side.
CompatibilityWorks with Meteor Client tools like HIG, which support widths up to 6 blocks.
AlignmentOdd-numbered width makes lining up easier than even-numbered highways.
ConsistencyPrevents constant width changes along long stretches of highway.

The Watchmen

Partnership

The Watchmen, founded by beast_machine, arrived on 6b6t in early 2025 after a long history on 2b2t. They partnered with THM following the establishment of the 5 Blocks Wide Rule, contributing massively to highway expansion: they expanded the Nether highways more than any other group in 6b6t's history, other than the highway men, constructed 23 member bases, and hosted giveaways with over 20,000 kits.

The Watchmen officially disbanded on April 5th, 2026.
